5 dishes to order

Trofie al pesto (€14–18) · Focaccia genovese (€2.50) · Focaccia di Recco (€8–12) · Fritto misto di mare (€15–28) · Farinata (€4). Buy focaccia hot from a bakery in the morning.

Portofino food warning: one of the priciest villages in Italy. Walk it, photograph it, drink one Aperol, eat in Santa Margherita or Camogli instead.

Three Budget Packages

Straight talk on 50,000 Kč: not realistic. Four people in separate rooms means two units every night, plus ~8,300 Kč fuel and ~3,000 Kč tolls before anyone eats. The honest floor, lean over 8 days, is ~85,000 Kč.

🟢 The Saver

~85K Kč / €3,4008 days · ~21,250 Kč per person
  • One-push drives (no Innsbruck nights)
  • Budget apartments, separate rooms
  • Self-cater breakfast + lunch
  • Free beaches, CT essentials
Recommended

🔵 The Balanced

~140K Kč / €5,66010 days · ~35,400 Kč per person
  • Overnight Innsbruck each way
  • Nice apartment / B&B, pool option
  • Mix of eating out + self-catering
  • All boats, CT card, Carrara, beach club

🟣 The Villa Dream

~225K Kč / €9,00010 days · ~56,250 Kč per person
