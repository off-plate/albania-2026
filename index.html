The big decision

Liguria, not Tuscany

You started thinking Tuscany. But Tuscany's own coast (Versilia, Forte dei Marmi) is flat, suburban and viewless. The drama you want lives one notch northwest, on the Ligurian Riviera, where the beach and the "wow" are the same place.

Base 1 · 3 nights

Levanto

The smart gateway to Cinque Terre: its own sandy beach, a train station 4 minutes from the villages, real parking, and prices ~40% below the villages themselves.

Base 2 · 4 nights

Santa Margherita Ligure

Five minutes from Portofino without Portofino's €400 rooms. A real harbour with boats to San Fruttuoso and Camogli, and the clearest water in Liguria at nearby Paraggi.

Why this order: coming south from Prague you hit Levanto first, then loop 1 hour north to Santa Margherita, which sits back toward the route home. Zero backtracking.
Getting there

The Drive: Prague → Levanto

~950 km via Austria and the Brenner Pass. Roughly 11 hours of pure driving, so the team's call is to split it with one overnight in Innsbruck each way (built into the 10-day plan). The Saver package does it in one push to save the night.

The 10-day plan

Day by Day

Suggested window: late August (after Ferragosto, Aug 15) for calmer crowds and lower prices. Dates are flexible — this is the shape.

1Sat
DRIVE · Prague → Innsbruck (~500 km)

Roll out early, Alps by evening

Leave Prague by 8am. Refuel at Ansfelden. Arrive Innsbruck mid-afternoon, walk the old town, easy dinner, sleep. Overnight: Innsbruck.

2Sun
DRIVE · Innsbruck → Levanto (~470 km)

Over the Brenner, feet in the sea

Fill up at Weer before Italy. Cross the Brenner, drop down to La Spezia and Levanto by early afternoon. Settle in, hit Levanto's own beach, first Ligurian dinner. Overnight: Levanto (1/3).

3Mon
CINQUE TERRE

The five villages, by train

Train from Levanto into Cinque Terre before 9am to beat the crush. Manarola & Vernazza viewpoints, the Via dell'Amore (pre-booked slot), swim stops, focaccia. Overnight: Levanto (2/3).

4Tue
CAR DAY · south of Levanto

Portovenere, Tellaro & white cliffs

Drive to Portovenere (church on the rock), the tiny car-free village of Tellaro, and optionally the hike to Punta Bianca's turquoise cove. Tourist-light, photo-heavy. Overnight: Levanto (3/3).

5Wed
TRANSFER · Levanto → S. Margherita (~1 h)

Move north, slow afternoon

Optional Carrara marble-quarry 4×4 detour en route. Arrive Santa Margherita, check in, sundowner at Paraggi cove. Overnight: S. Margherita (1/4).

6Thu
PORTOFINO

The piazzetta & lighthouse path

Boat or bus to Portofino, walk the headland to the lighthouse, swim at Paraggi. The iconic harbour shot. Overnight: S. Margherita (2/4).

7Fri
BOAT DAY

San Fruttuoso & Camogli

Boat to the hidden abbey-cove of San Fruttuoso (no road in), then the pastel fishing town of Camogli. Overnight: S. Margherita (3/4).

8Sat
SLOW DAY

Sestri Levante or pool day

Baia del Silenzio in Sestri Levante (free crescent beach, stunning at sunset) — or do nothing by the pool. Your call. Overnight: S. Margherita (4/4).

9Sun
DRIVE · S. Margherita → Innsbruck (~6 h)

Begin the loop home

Last swim, then drive back over the Brenner to Innsbruck for the night. Overnight: Innsbruck.

10Mon
DRIVE · Innsbruck → Prague (~5.5 h)

Home by evening

Easy final leg back to Prague.

The 8-day version: drop the two Innsbruck nights (one-push drives) and trim one beach day. Same highlights, lower cost — this is the Saver package below.

Three Budget Packages

Researched from real fuel, toll, room, food and activity prices. Converted at 1 € = 25 CZK. These are totals for the whole group of four.

Straight talk on the 50,000 CZK target: it isn't realistic for this trip. Four people in separate rooms for 8–10 days means paying for two units every night, plus ~€440 of fuel and tolls before anyone eats. The honest floor — done lean — is around 80,000 CZK. To go lower you'd have to share one room (breaks your rule), camp, or cut the trip to ~5 days. The packages below are built on real prices, not wishful ones.

Sasha's playbook

How to Spend Less Without Wrecking It

Self-cater breakfast + focaccia lunch

Saves ~€120–200 per couple over the trip. Dinner out stays the treat.

Levanto's free beach over a club

Saves ~€150–300 vs four days of sunbeds. Keep the paid beach for one Monterosso afternoon.

Dinner one block off the seafront

Same food, no harbour markup — ~€200–400 saved across five evenings.

Cinque Terre Treno Card, bought online

2-day card €61 beats singles at 4+ rides/day, and skips the August queues.

Fill the tank in Austria

Top up full at Weer before the Brenner — Italian motorway fuel is ~€0.40/L more.

One ferry ticket, two stops

The S. Margherita → San Fruttuoso return (€24) already includes the Portofino stop.

Reference prices: CT 2-day card €61 · Via dell'Amore €10 supplement · S.Margherita↔Portofino €12 · San Fruttuoso return €24 · Carrara 4×4 €15 · Levanto parking €15–24/day · lean food €35–45/person/day, comfortable €60–80.
