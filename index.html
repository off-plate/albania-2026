5 dishes to order

Trofie al pesto (€14–18) · Focaccia genovese (€2.50) · Focaccia di Recco (€8–12) · Fritto misto di mare (€15–28) · Farinata (€4). Buy focaccia hot from a bakery in the morning, never at a restaurant.

Portofino food warning: it's one of the most expensive villages in Italy (coffee €3–4, mains €30+, a sandwich cut in half can cost extra). Walk it, photograph it, drink one Aperol, then eat in Santa Margherita or Camogli for a fraction of the price.

The real numbers · group of 4 · everything in

Three Budget Packages

Straight talk on 50,000 Kč: it isn't realistic. Four people in separate rooms means paying for two units every night, plus ~8,300 Kč of fuel and ~3,000 Kč of tolls before anyone eats. The honest floor, done lean over 8 days, is around 85,000 Kč. To go lower you'd share one room (breaks your rule) or cut the trip to ~5 days.
