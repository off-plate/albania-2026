## DAY 1 — Prague → Innsbruck (~500 km, ~5h drive)

### Timing Skeleton
- **06:00** — Depart Prague. Beat city traffic.
- **08:00–08:30** — First fuel/coffee stop (Rozvadov/Waidhaus area, DE border).
- **11:30–12:30** — Munich ring-road or lunch stop (Holzkirchen area, avoid central Munich).
- **13:30** — Enter Austria, Innsbruck outskirts.
- **14:00** — Check in hotel (most allow early bag drop at minimum).
- **14:30–18:30** — Old Town walk: Maria-Theresien-Strasse, Goldenes Dachl, Court Church, Hofburg (exterior).
- **18:30** — Dinner in Altstadt. Try Weinstube or Tyrolean restaurant.
- **20:30** — Early night — long drive tomorrow.

### Transport
- Prague → Innsbruck via D5/E50 → A8 → A93 → A12. Tolls: Austria Vignette (~€9.90/10-day, buy at border or online). German Autobahn: free.
- Fuel: ~50L diesel for 500 km. Budget ~€80–90 (est.).
- Innsbruck city: walk everywhere.

### Parking
- Hotel parking: **€15/night** (ESTIMATE — confirm at booking). Many Innsbruck hotels offer garage/underground.
- Altstadt parking garages: €2–3/hr if hotel has none.

### Practical Notes
- Austria Vignette is mandatory — buy online at asfinag.at before departing or at first Austrian fuel stop.
- Innsbruck August temps: 24–28°C, pleasant evenings.
- Old Town is walkable and very compact; no car needed once parked.

## DAY 3 — Cinque Terre Full Day by Train from Levanto

### Timing Skeleton
- **07:45** — Walk to Levanto station (5 min). Buy/activate Cinque Terre Treno Cards.
- **08:04** — First CT Express to Monterosso (~8 min). Arrive before the crowds.
- **08:15–09:30** — Monterosso: old town, beach, Sant'Antonio chapel.
- **09:45** — Train to Vernazza.
- **09:55–11:30** — Vernazza: castle, harbor, main piazza. Best before 10:30.
- **11:45** — Train to Corniglia or walk the trail.
- **12:00–13:00** — Corniglia: quietest village. Stairway, viewpoints, lunch.
- **13:15** — Train to Manarola.
- **13:30–15:30** — Manarola: harbor swim, Via dell'Amore entry from Riomaggiore side. Book your time slot in advance.
- **14:00–14:30** — Walk Via dell'Amore (Riomaggiore → Manarola, ~1.2 km, 20–30 min).
- **15:30** — Riomaggiore: final drinks, swimming cove.
- **17:00** — Train back to Levanto.
- **18:00** — Back in Levanto, shower, dinner.

### Transport
- Cinque Terre Express (Levanto–La Spezia line): departs Levanto every **20–30 min** in summer. Journey Levanto→Monterosso: ~8 min.
- **Treno Card covers unlimited trains all day** — no separate tickets needed for each village.
- Via dell'Amore: **book time slot in advance** at viadellamore.info or park portal. Entry Riomaggiore side, one-way to Manarola in high season. Cap 400 pax/hour. Book 72h before.

### Tickets
- **Cinque Terre Treno Card, August weekday (Band B): €29.50/person** — CONFIRMED. If this falls on a weekend, Band C: €35.00.
- Via dell'Amore: **included in Treno Card since Jan 2026** — no supplement. Reserve time slot only.
- Buy cards at Levanto station (info point) or online at trenitalia.com in advance. August: pre-buy to avoid queues.

### Parking
- Car stays at Levanto lot: **€24/day** (Day 3 of 3-night stay).

### Practical Notes
- August is peak season — Vernazza and Manarola are most crowded 11:00–15:00. Start early.
- CT ferry is a good option for one leg (e.g., train there, ferry back from Riomaggiore to Levanto), adds scenery variety. Ferry day pass €42 if you want unlimited ferry hops.
- Corniglia has no ferry access — train only.
- Wear hats, carry water — shade is scarce on trails.
- Via dell'Amore time slots book up — do this when you book accommodation.

## DAY 4 — Car Day: Portovenere, Tellaro, Optional Punta Bianca Hike

### Timing Skeleton
- **08:30** — Depart Levanto by car. SP566 south ~40 min to La Spezia.
- **09:15** — Continue to **Portovenere** (~15 min from La Spezia). Park near boat landing.
- **09:30–12:00** — Portovenere: San Pietro church (free), Doria Castle (~€2.50/person), Arpaia grotto, harbor views, narrow caruggi. Buy fresh focaccia.
- **12:00–13:00** — Lunch in Portovenere (seafood, focus on pesce locale).
- **13:30** — Drive to **Tellaro** (~35 min via La Spezia and SP331 coast road).
- **14:00–15:30** — Tellaro: village stroll, octopus legend, church, swimming off rocks. Park in Via Fiascherino (blue lines, €1.50/h) or small lot 200m from centre.
- **15:30** — OPTIONAL: Drive to Punta Bianca (~20 min via Bocca di Magra). Free parking on roadside near Montemarcello junction. 20–30 min walk to white cliffs + sea.
- **17:00** — Return drive Levanto (~50 min).
- **18:00** — Back in Levanto. Final evening here.

### Transport
- Full loop Levanto→Portovenere→Tellaro→(Punta Bianca)→Levanto: ~120 km. Fuel: ~€15 est.
- Portovenere: no ZTL for private cars accessing main parking area (Piazzale Libertà or nearby), but VERY limited spaces in August. Arrive before 09:30 or risk full lots. Alternative: park in La Spezia and take ferry to Portovenere (Navigazione Golfo dei Poeti, ~€15 return/person — worth it to skip parking stress).
- Tellaro: park Via Fiascherino or Lerici and taxi last 2 km if lots full.

### Activity Costs
- Portovenere: San Pietro church free; Doria Castle **€2.50/person** (ESTIMATE — cash only, small booth).
- Tellaro: free to wander.
- Punta Bianca hike: free access (no entrance fee, no reservation needed). Easy moderate, 30 min walk, stunning white cliff beach.

### Parking
- Portovenere lot: ~€2/hr, max ~€16–20/day (ESTIMATE, summer rate). Or use La Spezia park + ferry.
- Levanto lot final day: **€24**.

## DAY 5 — Levanto → Santa Margherita Ligure (~70 km, ~1h) + Optional Carrara Detour

### Option A: Direct Drive to SML (no Carrara)
- **10:00** — Check out Levanto. SP566 north, A12, exit Santa Margherita Ligure. ~1h.
- **11:30** — Arrive SML, check in (or drop bags), lunch.
- **Afternoon** — Beach, harbor walk, Piazza Martiri della Libertà aperitivo.

### Option B: Carrara Quarry Detour (add ~2.5h, worth it)
- **08:30** — Check out Levanto. SP566 north to A12, then Carrara/Colonnata exit. ~1h 15 min.
- **10:00** — Arrive Carrara quarry area (Fantiscritti yard for main operator Carrara Marble Tour, open from 10:00).
- **10:00–13:00** — 4x4 jeep tour (3h including drive to quarry faces). No reservation needed for groups under 20, just show up. Next jeep departs within 20–30 min of arrival.
- **13:30** — Continue A12 north, exit Santa Margherita Ligure. ~1h.
- **15:00** — Arrive SML, check in, settle, late lunch/early dinner.

### Transport
- Levanto → Carrara → SML: ~180 km total. Fuel: ~€20.
- Motorway toll A12 (short section): ~€3–4 (ESTIMATE).
- Note: Carrara Marble Tour operator is at **Via Campo Cecina, Fantiscritti quarry** — GPS: 44.1028° N, 10.0934° E. No online booking needed for <20 pax; just drive up.

### Carrara Jeep Tour Cost
- Via LivTours/private operator: **€98/person** (3h, guide, snacks, private vehicle, confirmed price).
- Alternative direct-walk-up option at Carrara Marble Tour (carraramarbletour.it): price range **€15–30/person** for the basic shared jeep tour (unconfirmed exact price — call +39 0585 779673 to confirm). This is the "show up and ride" option without pre-booking.
- **Recommendation for group of 4**: Book LivTours at €98/person if you want a private experience with guide. If budget-sensitive, walk up to Carrara Marble Tour for ~€20–25/person.

### Parking at SML
- **Parcheggio Largo Giusti** or **Parcheggio Via Roma** — main public lots. Best for multi-day stay.
- Rate: **€18–25/day** in August (ESTIMATE based on Aug 2025 data; €1.50–2.50/hr).
- Many hotels include or heavily discount parking — confirm at booking.
- Leave car here for Days 5–8 (4 nights).

## DAY 6 — Portofino + Lighthouse Hike + Paraggi Beach

### Timing Skeleton
- **08:30** — Walk to SML harbor, buy return boat ticket to Portofino.
- **09:00** — Ferry departs SML to Portofino. ~15 min crossing.
- **09:15** — Arrive Portofino harbor. Village is quiet before 10:30.
- **09:30–10:30** — Portofino village stroll, harbor, Castello Brown exterior (€6/person if open, optional).
- **10:30–12:30** — Hike to **Faro di Portofino** (lighthouse). Trailhead from village center. 2.6 km, ~45 min one way, easy coastal trail. Views are spectacular. Small bar at lighthouse.
- **12:30–13:00** — Return hike to village.
- **13:00–14:30** — Lunch in Portofino (budget €20–30/person; this is the expensive one — consider bringing picnic).
- **14:30–17:30** — Walk or take 5-min boat back to **Paraggi** (Spiaggia di Paraggi). Public section free. Crystal-clear water. Stabilimento Paraggi for sunbeds (expensive, ~€30–50/person/day for a chair). Snorkeling optional.
- **17:30** — Take AMT bus Line 782 from Paraggi/Portofino back to SML (€5/person one way) OR return boat from Portofino (within return ticket validity, confirm with operator).
- **19:00** — Back SML, aperitivo on harbor promenade.

### Transport Options to Portofino
- **Boat from SML**: Return ticket **€20/person**. Most scenic. Departs ~hourly in summer from SML harbor (Servizio Marittimo del Tigullio).
- **Bus AMT Line 782**: €5 one way. All-electric. Runs frequently in summer. Tickets via AMT app or card on board. Scenic coastal road.
- **Recommended**: Boat there (€20 return, take early morning), bus 782 back from Portofino (€5) — saves €5/person and different views.
  - Actual cost: €12 (SML→Portofino one way) + €5 (bus back) = €17/person vs €20 return boat.

### Parking
- Car stays at SML lot. Day rate: €20–25 (Day 6 of 4-night stay — ESTIMATE).

### Activities
- Portofino village: free to wander.
- Lighthouse hike: free, no ticket.
- Castello Brown: **€6/person** (ESTIMATE — check availability; sometimes closed for events).
- Paraggi beach public section: free. Bring towels, snorkeling gear.
