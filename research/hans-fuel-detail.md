## 2. Price Hierarchy & Smart Strategy

**Cheapest to most expensive:**

```
CZ (€1.67)  <  Austria road avg (€1.73)  <  Austria motorway (€1.78–1.83)
            <  Italy national avg (€1.92)  <  Italy autostrada self (€1.94)
            <<  Italy autostrada servito (€2.09–2.19)  ← NEVER USE THIS
```

### Strategy

1. **Fill to full in Prague (CZ)** before departure — cheapest fuel of the trip.
2. **Top up full at Ansfelden Nord or equivalent Austrian station before the Brenner** — prices jump hard once you enter Italy. Fill here, not at the Brenner itself (Italian side).
3. **In Italy: use self-service ONLY (fai da te / self).** Never press the attended/"servito" button — you pay €0.15–0.25/L extra for someone to hold the nozzle.
4. **Avoid Italian autostrada stations** where possible — use Trento Nord or Campogalliano exits to refuel at cheaper town pumps if time allows (saves ~€0.20–0.30/L vs. autostrada).
5. **Fill up full before re-entering Austria** at your last Italian stop to avoid paying Austrian prices for more km than needed.

### Quantified saving: Czech vs. Italian autostrada

Filling 60 litres in Czech Republic vs. Italian autostrada self-service:
- CZ: 60 × €1.67 = **€100.20**
- IT autostrada self: 60 × €1.94 = **€116.40**
- **Saving per fill: €16.20 (405 CZK)**

Filling 60 litres in Czech Republic vs. Italian autostrada servito:
- IT autostrada servito (€2.14 mid): 60 × €2.14 = **€128.40**
- **Saving per fill: €28.20 (705 CZK)**

## 3. Practical Range and Refuel Plan

**Tank:** 71 L. At 9.5 L/100 km, usable fill (~60 L from reserve start) gives **~630 km range**.

Full tank from Prague: up to ~700 km theoretical, but don't run to reserve. Plan refuels at ~500–600 km intervals.

### One-Way Route: Prague → Levanto (~950 km)

```
Prague  →  Ansfelden  →  Innsbruck/Brenner area  →  Trento  →  Levanto
  0 km        ~320 km           ~490 km               ~590 km    ~950 km
```

**Stop 1: Fill full in Prague (0 km)**
- With 71 L tank, you can reach Ansfelden (320 km) with ~35 L used, and still have 36 L.
- Alternatively carry full tank to Salzburg/Ansfelden area comfortably.

**Stop 2: Ansfelden Nord or similar Austrian A1 station (~320 km)**
- You used ~30 L. Refuel ~35–45 L to top up.
- This is the strategic fill before Italy — Austrian prices are €0.20–0.25/L cheaper than Italian autostrada.
- Alternative: if you prefer to push, stop at **Innsbruck West / Autogrill Weer** (~490 km, A12) — still Austria, still good price.

**Stop 3 (optional/emergency): Trento Nord exit (~590 km from Prague)**
- If you only topped up lightly in Austria, stop here. Town pumps at Trento Nord/Campogalliano save ~€0.20–0.30/L vs. autostrada.
- If you filled fully at Ansfelden (~350 km prior), you likely have 33+ L left (enough to reach Levanto at 360 km further).

**Conclusion one-way: 1–2 fill-ups, ideally 2 (Prague + Austria).**

### Return: Levanto → Prague (~950 km)

**Stop 1: Fill full in Levanto / Santa Margherita (0 km of return)**
- Use a town pump, not autostrada (saves ~€0.20–0.30/L).
- From Levanto to Austrian border via A7/A26 is ~360 km. Top up at Campogalliano exit (~230 km) if you didn't fill fully at start.

**Stop 2: Campogalliano / Modena area (~230 km into return, A1)**
- Large Autogrill complex with self-service pumps.
- Alternatively push to Austrian border at Brenner (~430 km) — possible if full tank.

**Stop 3: Weer Autogrill or Kufstein area (Austria, A12, ~490 km into return)**
- Final Austria top-up before Czech border.
- Czech prices are cheaper, so don't fill more than necessary here — get enough to reach a Czech station.

**Stop 4: Czech border area or Prague (~950 km)**
- Optional top-up in CZ at cheapest price for the remaining range.

**Conclusion return: 2–3 fill-ups.**

**Overall round trip: 3–4 fill-ups total.** Smart strategy = 4 planned stops.

## 5. Total Round-Trip Fuel Cost — Formula

**Total km:** 1,900 km  
**Consumption:** 9.5 L / 100 km  
**Total litres needed:** 1,900 × 9.5 / 100 = **180.5 L**

**Blended price (smart strategy):**
- ~65% Czech/Austrian: €1.72/L blended
- ~35% Italian autostrada self: €1.94/L
- Blended: (0.65 × 1.72) + (0.35 × 1.94) = 1.118 + 0.679 = **€1.797/L**

**Total cost formula:**
```
1,900 km × 9.5 L/100 km × €1.797/L = 180.5 L × €1.797 = €324.40
```

**In CZK:** €324.40 × 25 = **8,110 CZK**

Rounded to realistic (includes slight motorway overconsumption buffer): **~€330–350 / ~8,200–8,750 CZK**

### Comparison: what if you used only Italian autostrada servito (worst case)

```
180.5 L × €2.14 = €386.27 → 9,657 CZK
```

**Smart strategy saves: ~€55–60 (1,375–1,500 CZK) vs. worst-case Italian servito.**
