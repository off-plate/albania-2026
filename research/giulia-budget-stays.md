## BASE 1: CINQUE TERRE ACCESS (~2–3 nights)

**Best town: Levanto** — it's ON the Cinque Terre train line. From Levanto station you're in Monterosso al Mare in 5 minutes, Vernazza in 10, Riomaggiore in ~20. Trains run every 20 min. This is the single best budget base for Cinque Terre: real beach, real town, cheaper than the CT villages themselves, easy parking.

### Budget alternative: Reggimonti / Bonassola (~10 km south of Levanto)

If Levanto is fully booked or too expensive in August, look at **Bonassola** (next village south, also on the CT train line) or the inland hamlet **Reggimonti** above it.

- Bonassola: quieter village, own small beach, direct train to CT (~8 min to Levanto, then CT). 2-bed apartments found from **~€100–130/night base rate**, probably **€130–160/night in August**.

## BASE 2: PORTOFINO / TIGULLIO ACCESS (~4–5 nights)

**Best towns: Chiavari or Lavagna** — Both are dramatically cheaper than Santa Margherita Ligure (~€350+/night territory). They sit on the same Gulf of Tigullio coastline and have direct ferry access to Portofino (see ferry details below).

**Key logistics:**
- Chiavari to Portofino by car: 22 km, ~22 minutes
- Chiavari to Portofino by ferry (Line 2): departs 9:30 AM and 2:45 PM from Chiavari pier. Adult return: **€20** — this is the best way to visit Portofino (no parking nightmare)
- Lavagna to Portofino: same ferry (Lavagna is the stop before Chiavari on Line 2), same price
- Sestri Levante to Portofino: 33 km, ~33 min drive (or ferry from Sestri Levante)
- Chiavari own beach: Lido beach, 5 min walk from town centre
- Lavagna own beach: Lavagna Beach + La Goletta, walking distance from any central apartment

## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR BOOKING

1. **Book SOON.** August in Liguria sells out. The listings at €128/night base rate will be gone or repriced by July.
2. **Minimum stay.** Many August listings require 5–7 night minimum. Splitting into 2 bases (3+5 nights) satisfies most minimums.
3. **AC matters.** August on this coast is hot. Double-check AC is confirmed, not just "listed." Casa Camuri (Option 2, Levanto) did NOT confirm AC — check before booking.
4. **Parking in Levanto.** The town uses a paid parking pass system (€5/day) — easy and cheap for the XC90. No garage needed.
5. **Prices marked ESTIMATE** reflect the base rate found + known August premium (~30–50%). Get exact August quotes from hosts before committing.
6. **Ferry vs. drive to Portofino.** Drive is 22 min from Chiavari, but Portofino has almost no parking. Take the ferry (€20 return, Chiavari pier → Portofino → San Fruttuoso). Book ferry tickets in advance in summer.
