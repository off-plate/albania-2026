## 5. PARKING & ZTL

### Levanto Parking — CONFIRMED

*Source: [Visit Levanto](https://www.visitlevanto.it/en/blog/dove-parcheggiare-a-levanto/) / [levantemultiservizi.it](https://levantemultiservizi.it/parcheggi/)*

| Car Park | Hourly Rate | Daily Rate (24h) |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| P9/P9a/P10 Lungomare (near beach) | €2.00/hr | **€24.00/day** |
| Near railway station | €2.00/hr | **€15.00/day** |
| P13 / P14 historic centre area | €0.60–€1.50/hr | ~€7–€15/day |

> **Best option:** Park at the station or P9 near the beach (€15–€24/day). Levanto is walkable once you park. Leave the XC90 here for all Cinque Terre days — take the train.

### Santa Margherita Ligure Parking — CONFIRMED (approx)

*Source: [G7 Parking / Parkopedia](https://www.parkme.com/santa-margherita-ligure-it-parking)*

| Car Park | Rate |
|----------|------|
| G7 Parking Riviera (covered garage) | **~€30/day** |
| Open car parks (Vittorio Veneto, Miramare, 180+ spaces) | **€1.50–€2.50/hr (~€18–€25/day)** |
| Cheaper garage ~15 min walk | ~€27/night |

> **Best option for SML:** Arrive, park in Vittorio Veneto (180 spaces) or Miramare, and walk. Leave the car parked for the duration of your SML stay — boat or taxi to Portofino, no need to move it.

### ZTL WARNING — READ THIS

- All 5 Cinque Terre villages have strict ZTL zones. Cars are **banned from entering** the historic centres.
- Santa Margherita Ligure has ZTL cameras on the waterfront/historic centre approach.
- **Fines: €80–€300 per entry.** Cameras are automatic. A rental car's plate is logged and the fine follows you home (often with an admin surcharge from the rental company: €30–€50 extra).
- **Multiple entries in one day = multiple fines.** A tourist has accidentally racked up 4+ fines in an hour.
- **Strategy for XC90:** Park outside the ZTL on arrival, walk everywhere, use boats/trains. Do NOT attempt to drive into any Cinque Terre village or the SML seafront zone.
- Portofino: **virtually no car access** unless you're a resident. Park at Santa Margherita and boat or taxi in.

## 6. CARRARA MARBLE QUARRY TOUR

*Operator: [Carrara Marble Tour](https://www.carraramarbletour.it/tours/) — CONFIRMED*

| Tour | Price/Adult | Price/Child | Duration |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Basic 4x4 quarry tour (Piazzale Fantiscritti) | **€15.00** | €7.50 (under 6) | 50–60 min |
| Full City + Quarry Jeep Tour (hotel pickup, tasting, city tour) | **€65.00** | €25 (6–12), €15 (under 6) | 3–4 hours |

> **For 4 adults:**
> - Basic tour: **€60 total** (no reservation needed for groups under 20 — just show up at Piazzale Fantiscritti; tours depart every 20–30 min)
> - Full jeep day tour: **€260 total** (includes "Cavatore's breakfast" tasting, all transport, 2 stops)

> **Recommendation:** The €15 basic 4x4 is genuinely impressive and leaves time for exploring Carrara town and the famous marble market on your own. Full tour worth it if you want a guided deep-dive. Book full tour via website for August; basic tour walk-up is fine.

> Carrara is a detour off the A12 (about 30 min south of Levanto) — logical as a driving-day stop en route.

## 7. SAVINGS PLAYBOOK

### Top 10 Concrete Ways to Cut Cost on This Trip

| # | Action | EUR Impact |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **1. Self-cater breakfast daily** | Buy focaccia + coffee supplies at Conad/Coop. Eat on the terrace. Skip restaurant breakfast. | Save **€15–€25/person/day** vs cafe — ~**€120–€200** over the trip for 4 people |
| **2. Pack focaccia lunches for beach/trail days** | A focacceria lunch for 4 costs €24–€40. Restaurant lunch = €60–€80. | Save **€20–€40 per lunch day** — 5 days = **€100–€200 saved** |
| **3. Buy Cinque Terre Train Card online, not at station** | Avoids queuing in August heat. Same price but saves 30–45 min per person. No EUR saving but massive time saving. | £0 saved but prevents frustration/lost time |
| **4. Train Card vs. singles in August** | Zone C singles = €10 each. 4 singles in a day = €40. 1-day card = €35. 3+ journeys = card wins. 4 people, 1-day card each = €140 vs €160+ in singles for 4 rides each. | **€20–€40 per CT day** |
| **5. Use Levanto's free public beach** | Stabilimento for 4 = €50–€100/day. Levanto public sand = €0. Reserve ONE paid beach day for Monterosso as a treat. | **€150–€300 over the trip** (4 beach days free vs paid) |
| **6. Fill fuel in Austria on the drive down** | Austria petrol ~€1.51/L vs Italy €1.71/L. XC90 tank ~70L. Fill up in Innsbruck/Salzburg area before entering Italy. | **€14 saved per full tank** — do it both ways = ~**€28 total** (small but free money) |
| **7. Eat dinner one block back from the seafront** | Harbour-front restaurants add €10–€20pp coperto/markup vs identical food 100m away. | **€40–€80 saved per dinner for 4** — 5 dinners = **€200–€400** |
| **8. Book accommodation with free cancellation now** | August Levanto/SML books out. Lock in August dates now at current prices; refundable rooms allow adjustment. Late booking in July risks 2–3x price spikes or no availability. | Potentially **€200–€600** cheaper vs last-minute |
| **9. Use the SMdL–San Fruttuoso return ticket to see Portofino** | €24 return from SMdL includes a Portofino stop — no need to buy a separate SMdL–Portofino ticket (€20). See both destinations on one ticket. | **€16 saved for 4 people** on the Portofino/San Fruttuoso combo day |
| **10. Basic Carrara quarry tour walk-up (€15) vs. full jeep day tour (€65)** | The basic 4x4 is a genuine quarry experience. Full tour adds city tour + breakfast but isn't necessary. | **€200 saved for 4 people** (€60 vs €260) |

## 8. WHAT TO BOOK NOW vs. LATER

### Book NOW (June/July 2026 at the absolute latest)

| Item | Why | Where |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Accommodation in Levanto (Aug nights)** | August is fully booked by July. Levanto prices spike 40–60% late. Book refundable rates. | Booking.com / direct hotel |
| **Accommodation in Santa Margherita Ligure** | Same — SML and Portofino area fills fast. The good mid-range places go first. | Booking.com / Airbnb |
| **Via dell'Amore slots** | 200 people per 30-min slot. Sells out weeks ahead in August. | [viadellamore.info](https://www.viadellamore.info) |
| **Cinque Terre Train Cards** | No capacity limit but saves queuing. Can buy day-of but skip the station crush. | card.parconazionale5terre.it |

### Book 1–2 Weeks Before / On Arrival

|------|-----|

| Carrara basic quarry tour | Walk-up, no reservation needed for 4 people |
| Stabilimento sunbeds at Monterosso | Call ahead 1–2 days or walk in early morning |
| Restaurant reservations (popular spots) | For 4 people, book 1–2 days ahead at minimum for evenings |

|------|
