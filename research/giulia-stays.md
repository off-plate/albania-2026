## HONEST CONTEXT: The Villa-With-Pool Question

The dream of a **private villa with pool directly in Santa Margherita Ligure** is genuinely rare. SML is a built-up coastal town — not a villa-dotted hillside. What exists:

- Villas with private pools are almost exclusively in the hills above SML (San Lorenzo della Costa, San Michele di Pagana) or in adjacent towns (Rapallo, Zoagli, Recco). A 5-15 min drive to SML waterfront.
- Those that exist are typically 4-5 bedrooms and priced accordingly (€4,000-€12,000+/week).
- **True 2-bedroom private-pool villas in the immediate SML area are very rare** — most 2-bed places have no pool, or share one. Pool + 2 beds = either pay premium or compromise on location.
- **The realistic sweet spot:** A quality 2-bed apartment IN SML (walking distance to sea) + a hotel pool day pass, OR a hillside villa WITH pool 10 min drive from SML.

All options below are REAL, named, bookable properties. Prices marked ESTIMATE where August 2026 rates weren't directly confirmed; CONFIRMED where directly verified.

# BASE 2: SANTA MARGHERITA LIGURE (SML)
**Stay:** ~4-5 nights (use 5 nights for all price calculations = Aug 11-16 example)
**Goal:** 2 separate bedrooms. Michael's dream: private villa with pool. Parking essential.
**Parking reality in SML:** Brutal in peak August. The seafront is pedestrianized. Most hotels have private/garage parking. Self-parking on street is very limited. For any apartment without private parking, expect to use the Parcheggio Piazza Bo or Piazza Vittorio Veneto (paid, €2-3/hr or €20-25/day).

## THE POOL-VILLA REALITY CHECK

True 2-bed private-pool villas IN SML essentially don't exist on the short-term rental market. Here's what you get as you widen the search:

- **In SML proper:** Apartments (no pool, unless building has shared rooftop/terrace)
- **San Lorenzo della Costa / San Michele di Pagana (5-10 min drive uphill):** Small number of private villas with pool — typically 3-5 bedrooms
- **Rapallo / Recco (15 min drive):** More villa options, some with 2 bedrooms + pool
- **Verdict:** The 2-bed private-pool option EXISTS but is rare and costs €500-900+/night. More realistic at the budget/mid end is a quality apartment IN SML.

# BOOKING PRESSURE — AUGUST REALITY

**How far ahead?**
- Budget apartments (Giulia 2, Mondo Per Te): Book by **March-April 2026** — central 2-bed options in peak CT/SML go fast
- Mid-tier hotels (Palazzo Vannoni): Book **February-March 2026** — SML/Levanto hotels sell out completely in July/August
- Premium villas (Dolcina, Aldo): Contact **NOW (June 2026)** or you've likely missed August. Luxury Ligurian villas are often booked 12 months in advance
- Agriturismo Villanova: Book **by January 2026** for August. It's TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice and books fast

**What books out first in August:**
1. Any property with private parking (huge premium, scarce in SML)
2. Pool access at coastal locations
3. 2-bed configurations for 4 adults (most listings are 1-bed or 4-bed; 2-bed is the awkward middle)
