# Locations — Master List

This is the single source of truth. Michael sends locations/updates; I log them here, structured. The map gets built from this.

**How to send me a location:** anything works — a name, a Google Maps link, a paste of "lat, lng", a screenshot, or just "add X near Y". I'll geocode it and slot it in.

---

## Format (per location)
| # | Name | Category | Area / Group | Coordinates | Source link | Notes |
|---|------|----------|--------------|-------------|-------------|-------|

Categories are flexible — e.g. sleep, beach, view, food, must-see, maybe, drive-stop.
